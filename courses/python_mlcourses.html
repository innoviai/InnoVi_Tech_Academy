Abstract

This course examines advanced concepts and recent developments in machine learning. The course will consist of a mix of lectures, to introduce advanced concepts, and student-led seminars, for discussing recent developments. At the end of the course, students will have working knowledge of as well as practical experience in advanced machine learning. This is a topics course and the course syllabus will be adjusted to fit the interests of the students

On completion of the module, learners are expected to be able to:

  • Present the basic concepts and models of machine learning.
  • Introduce the latest machine learning technology with impactful applications in NLP and computer vision.
  • Understand advanced machine learning algorithms and how they are derived.
  • Apply advanced machine learning algorithms to solve real-world problems.
